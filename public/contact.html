Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book appointments through our Patient Portal, by calling +94 11 123 4568, or by visiting any of our branches in person.

What should I bring for my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), previous medical records, and a list of current medications.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us to verify your specific coverage.

What are your visiting hours?

General visiting hours are 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM daily. ICU visiting hours may differ - please check with the nursing staff.
